Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Friday ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

The App operates primarily as a local-first application. We collect only the information necessary to provide voice recognition, speech synthesis, and weather services.

  • Voice Input: When you use voice commands, audio is processed locally in your browser or by your device's Web Speech API. The App does not record or store audio locally unless explicitly saved by you.
  • Location Data: The App requests approximate location to provide localized weather information. This is used only to fetch weather data and is not stored or transmitted to third parties beyond the weather API.
  • Speech Synthesis: Text-to-speech uses your device's native speech engine. The App does not send this data to external servers.
  • Local Storage: The App may cache minimal data locally (browser cache, local storage) to improve performance. This data remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo API: Provides weather data. Open-Meteo does not require authentication and does not collect personal data beyond the IP address used in the API request (standard for any web service). See their privacy policy at https://open-meteo.com/en/privacy
  • Google Fonts: The App loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect limited data about font requests. See https://fonts.google.com/metadata/icons

How We Use Information

  • To enable voice recognition and speech synthesis features
  • To provide localized weather information
  • To improve App performance and functionality
  • To provide customer support

Data Retention

The App does not retain personal data on remote servers. Data stored locally on your device persists until you clear your browser cache or uninstall the App.

Security

The App runs entirely in your browser (PWA) and does not transmit personal information to our servers. All data remains on your device. When the App communicates with third-party services (weather API), these connections use HTTPS encryption.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your local data by clearing browser storage
  • Disable location access through your device settings
  • Deny microphone permission at any time through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store app listing or open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Compliance

This App complies with Google Play's policies on user privacy and does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. The App is designed with privacy by default.
